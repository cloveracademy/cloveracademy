About Us

We provide after-school and weekend school enrichment programs for talented students from grades IV - XII. we also provide as well as students who welcome academic challenge. Our courses will help prepare your child for a variety of regional, national, and international contests including math competitions (AMC/AIME/USAJMO/USAMO, MathCounts, MathLeague, MOEMS, CML, Math Kangaroo, etc.), Writing Contests, Science Olympiads (Chemistry, Biology, Physics, General Science, etc), Spelling Bees, and Debating Contests, as well as Extracurricular activities (Programming Olympiad, Astronomy Olympiad, Amateur Radio League, Chess Tournaments, etc). We are a boutique consulting firm with specialization in IT related Human Capital Management solutions and advanced technology solutions including distributed computing, cloud computing and search solutions. Our expertise in both Information Technology as architects and developers and our human resource management ventures in IT field as OptCircle.com and H1B360.com has given us an unique perspective to this market.

We advise both IT and Non IT sectors and firms of all size in issues related to technology resource management. The various models that we offer are "Embedded Resource Management" solution where our expert team will be deployed on site to develop an effective solution for the consulting practice, "Remote Resource Management" solution where we deploy a team offsite to custom support your needs or a "Preferred Vendor" solution where we work on demand to fulfill your Information Technology workers demand.

We architect, build and deploy solutions that is unique to each customer based on their requirements. We are well versed in Java, .Net and PHP based solutions.Some of the tools that we use for its versatility and simplicity in solving problems are JQuery, Lucene and Tomcat. We enjoy technology challenges and we work with our customers in finding solutions that enhances their core business proposition.
