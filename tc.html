Terms & Conditions

By enrolling into classes/courses offered by the Clover Academy (CA), the Applicant acknowledges and agrees to the Terms and Conditions as specified below:

Enrollment:

  • Enrollment will be done using this paper based form until it is online process starts.
  • Enrollment in CA Courses may be subject to pre-course assessment to determine Applicant’s suitability to enroll in lessons. CA reserves the right to refuse the admission of any Applicant who, in its opinion, does not meet the minimum requirement for courses and/or may impact the learning of other Applicant’s at the center.
  • CA reserves the right to discontinue an Applicant’s enrollment at any time due to non-payment of the Course Fees, disruptive behavior in class or at the center, or negative impact on other Applicants’ learning.
  • CA reserves the right to change the class instructor as deemed appropriate or necessary and is not required to provide prior notice for such arrangements.

    • Fees and Payments:

  • All Course Fees must be paid in advance of the Applicant attending CA’s classes. CA reserves the right to prevent an Applicant attending class if Course Fees have not been paid.
  • Sometimes an Early Bird Discount is applicable, but it changes session to session and details will be available on website. Fees must be received in full on or before the specified Early Bird Discount due date to receive the discount.
  • All Course Fees are non-refundable once the Applicant has been sent the Confirmation Enrollment Email, with exceptions of the circumstances listed under Refund Policy.
  • Course Fees must be paid on a monthly basis.
  • Course Fees for the first month, must be paid within one week of CA sending the Confirmation Enrollment Email.
  • After the first month, Course Fees must be paid no later than the commencement of the Applicant’s first class of the month.
  • Fees must be paid in Check payable to Clover Academy. There is a $25 returned check fee if payment check is returned by the bank.

    • Refund Policy:

  • In the case of CA class closure prior to the commencement of a course or class, we will refund in full the outstanding Course Fee to the Applicant immediately.
  • There is no refund for CA classes and Courses confirmed by CA. However, in the event that:
    • a confirmed class/ Course needs to be canceled; or
    • a confirmed class/course which has commenced ceases to continue, and the Applicant declines the revised arrangements offered by CA.
  • CA will refund in full or on a pro-rata basis the Course Fee collected as soon as possible and in any event no later than one month after the notification date of the class / Course cancellation.

    • Refund Procedures:

  • We will inform the Applicant of refund arrangements in writing (e-mail, fax or letter).
  • We will refund Course Fee to the Applicant in accordance to the Refund Policy.
  • We will issue a refund receipt to the Applicant and the Applicant is required to sign to acknowledge receipt of the refunded payment.
  • Refunds will be paid in the form of check payment only.

    • Student’s/Parent’s Responsibilities:

      The Student and Parents agree:
  • not to reproduce, distribute or show to any other person, in whole or in part, any of the class materials, lesson notes, assessments and tests distributed by CA without the prior written consent of CA.
  • not to solicit or make private arrangements for tuition with instructors/tutors employed/contracted by CA, without prior consent of CA;
  • to abide by the CA Code of Conduct;
  • check emails regularly for notifications from CA;
  • to attend all classes of their enrolled CA Courses in order to gain the most benefit from the classes or courses.

    • Adverse Weather Conditions Policy:

  • Classes will not be held when there is inclement weather (e.g. for snow or other risky road conditions). There will be rescheduling but no refund for cancelled classes due to weather. Every attempt will be made to make up the cancelled classes. For Class cancellation due to bad weather whether or not a class is cancelled, please check our Website.
